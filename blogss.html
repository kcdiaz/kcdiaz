    The History of Yahoo!

    January 31, 2022

    Yahoo!, Inc. is a digital media firm that uses research, communications, and digital content offerings to inform, connect, and entertain users. Americas; Europe, Middle East, and Africa (EMEA); and Asia Pacific are the three geographical segments across which it operates. Jerry Chih-Yuan Yang and David Filo launched the company in January 1994, and it is based in Sunnyvale, California.

    Inclusiveness in Design

    Febraury 21, 2022

    In terms of design, inclusivity refers to creating products that are equally beneficial and inclusive of all people, regardless of their personal differences. We must examine the outliers while contemplating inclusion. What should we do when designing for colorblind people? How do you account for age differences in your designs? When it comes to designing your product, everyone should be treated equally.
